Lexus Rx 350

The Lexus RX 350 is a popular mid-size luxury SUV known for its comfort, reliability, and refined driving experience, featuring powerful yet efficient turbocharged engines (like the new 275hp 2.4L turbo) and available with FWD or AWD, offering advanced tech, premium interiors, and hybrid options in newer models for improved fuel economy, available across various model years with prices varying significantly based on age, condition, and features.This video provides a detailed review of the 2026 Lexus RX 350:

Key Characteristics

  • Engine: Modern RX 350s often feature a 2.4L turbocharged four-cylinder engine, delivering 275 horsepower, while hybrid (RX 350h) and older models offer different powertrains.
  • Performance: Paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission, it provides smooth acceleration and a comfortable ride, with AWD available for enhanced traction.
  • Interior: Expect luxury features like synthetic leather (NuLuxe), heated front seats, dual-zone climate control, large touchscreens (9.8-inch standard), and wireless Apple CarPlay/Android Auto in recent models.
  • Technology: Includes the "Hey Lexus" voice assistant, multiple USB ports, premium audio systems, and available safety features like pre-collision systems.
  • Trims: Available in various trims, including the sporty F SPORT, offering enhanced design and performance.

Models & Generations

  • Newer Generations (e.g., 2020+): Focus on bold designs, sophisticated tech, and the powerful turbo engine.
  • Older Generations (e.g., 2000s-2010s): Known for legendary Lexus reliability, often found used with V6 engines, offering great value.
